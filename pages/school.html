Bikini Bottom University

Bikini Bottom University is a level located in the city of Bikini Bottom. It appears in the episode "Salsa Imbecilicus." It was built by and is run by Sandy and Karen. The university is the shape of a college graduation hat. It appears to be made of black brick. It has a porthole above the front door with what appears to be a bell hanging in the center.
