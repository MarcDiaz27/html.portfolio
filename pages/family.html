SqurePants Family

Grandpa SquarePants

Grandpa SquarePants was the father of Harold, Sherm, Blue SquarePants, and Grandma SquarePants' husband. He was also the paternal grandfather of SpongeBob, Todd, BlackJack, Stanley, and possibly Larry, Margaret's father-in-law, a descendant of SpongeBuck, Primitive Sponge, and SpongeGar, one of SpongeTron's ancestors. In the future, he will have a great great grandson.

Grandma SquarePants

Grandma SquarePants is the widow of Grandpa SquarePants, the mother of Harold, Sherm, and Captain Blue SquarePants. She is also the paternal grandmother of SpongeBob, Todd, BlackJack, Stanley, and Possibly Larry, Margaret's mother-in-law, and one of SpongeTron's ancestors. In the Future, she will be a great great grandmother.

Harold SquarePants

Harold SquarePants is SpongeBob's father, the son of Grandma and Grandpa SquarePants, uncle of Stanley, BlackJack, Larry, and Todd SquarePants, brother of Sherm and Blue SquarePants, Margaret's husband, a descendant of SpongeBuck, Primitive Sponge, and SpongeGar, and one of SpongeTron's ancestors. In the future, he will be a great-grandfather.

Margaret SquarePants

Margaret SquarePants is Harold's wife, SpongeBob's mother, daughter-in-law of Grandpa and Grandma SquarePants, sister-in-law of Captain Blue and Sherm, the aunt of Larry, Todd, BlackJack, and Stanley, and one of SpongeTron's ancestors. In the future, she will be a great-grandmother.

Sherm SquarePants

Sherman "Sherm" SquarePants is the brother of Harold and Blue, a son of Grandma and Grandpa SquarePants, Stanley's father, the uncle of SpongeBob, BlackJack, Todd, and possibly Larry, a brother-in-law of Margaret, and a descendant of SpongeBuck, Primitive Sponge, and SpongeGar.

Blue SquarePants

Blue SquarePants is a son of Grandma and Grandpa SquarePants. He is the brother of Harold and Sherm SquarePants, the uncle of SpongeBob, Blackjack, Todd, Stanley, and possibly Larry, a brother-in-law of Margaret, and a descendant of SpongeBuck, Primitive Sponge, and SpongeGar.

Stanley S. SquarePants

Stanley S. SquarePants is Sherm's son, the cousin of SpongeBob, BlackJack, Todd, and possibly Larry a nephew of Blue, Harold, and Margaret, a grandson of Grandpa and Grandma SquarePants, and a descendant of SpongeBuck, Primitive Sponge, and SpongeGar. In the future, he will be a first cousin 2x removed.

BlackJack SquarePants

BlackJack SquarePants is the cousin of SpongeBob, Stanley, Todd, and possibly Larry, a nephew of Blue, Sherm, Harold, and Margaret, a grandson of Grandpa and Grandma SquarePants, and a descendant of SpongeBuck, Primitive Sponge, and SpongeGar. In the future, he will be a first cousin 2x removed.

Todd SquarePants

Todd SquarePants is the cousin of SpongeBob, Stanley, BlackJack, and possibly Larry, a nephew of Blue, Sherm, Harold, and Margaret, a grandson of Grandpa and Grandma SquarePants, a descendant of SpongeBuck, Primitive Sponge, and SpongeGar.

Primitive Sponge

In "SB-129," Squidward accidentally travels to early prehistoric times, where he meets early ancestors of SpongeBob and Patrick. This primitive sponge has little in common with his modern counterpart, being a savage and unintelligent creature with no discernible spoken language.

SpongeGar

SpongeGar was a prehistoric ancestor of SpongeBob, SpongeBuck, SpongeTron, BlackJack, Harold, Stanley, Blue, Todd, Sherm, Grandpa SquarePants, and possibly Larry. seen in "Ugh." SpongeGar and Patar are distinct from the Neanderthals seen in "SB-129," more closely resembling their modern-day counterparts and having developed a simple language.

SpongeBuck SquarePants

SpongeBuck SquarePants was the western ancestor of Grandpa SquarePants, Harold, Blue, Sherm, SpongeBob, BlackJack, Todd, Stanley, possibly Larry and SpongeTron. He appeared in "Pest of the West." SpongeBuck moved to Dead Eye Gulch, where he was seeking a job. He was immediately hired as the sheriff. He stood up to Dead Eye Plankton, a wealthy citizen who owned almost all the land in the town.

Spongebob Squarepants

Spongebob Squarepants is a talking square sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea. His relentless cheerfulness gets on the nerves of his grumpy neighbor, Squidward Tentacles. Spongebob works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, owned by the kind but greedy Mr. Krabs.

SpongeBob's grandson

SpongeBob's grandson appears in the episode "The Great Patty Caper," where he was shown 75 years into the future. He heard the story of Great Patty Caper that his Grandfather told him, though entirely ignored his SpongeBob's story by playing a video game and saying, "I beat my high score.

SpongeTron

SpongeTron will be a descendant of SpongeBob, Harold, Margaret, SpongeBuck, SpongeGar, Primitive Sponge, Grandpa SquarePants, and Grandma SquarePants. He will live in the 41st Century.
